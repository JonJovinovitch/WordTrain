PRIVACY POLICY

WORD TRAIN — DISCORD ACTIVITY · LAST UPDATED JULY 14, 2026

Word Train is a game you play inside Discord. This policy explains exactly what it stores, why, and how to get it deleted. It's short because the game collects very little.

1. What we collect

When you launch Word Train, Discord asks you to authorize it. We request the identify scope only. From that, and from the activity session, we store:

  • Your Discord user ID — a number that identifies your account
  • Your display name and avatar image URL — so the leaderboard shows who you are
  • The Discord server ID where you played — so each server has its own leaderboard
  • Your game results — score, which map, and when you played

That's the whole list. We do not request or receive your email address, your password, your messages, your DMs, your voice or video, your friends list, or the list of servers you belong to.

2. Why we collect it

Only to run the leaderboard: to show your name and score, to keep one entry per player (your personal best), and to keep each Discord server's board separate. That's it — there is no other use.

3. What we don't do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone.
  • We do not show ads or run advertising trackers.
  • We do not build profiles of you or track you across other sites.
  • We do not read anything in your Discord server.

4. Where it's stored

Scores are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway, which hosts the game itself. Traffic is encrypted over HTTPS.

5. Who else is involved

  • Discord — confirms who you are and runs the activity. Your use of Discord is covered by Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — hosts the game and its database.

Your avatar image is loaded directly from Discord's servers.

6. How long we keep it

Leaderboard entries are kept while the game runs, so scores persist between sessions. You can ask us to delete yours at any time (see below), and we'll remove your scores and identifying details.

7. Deleting your data

Contact us and ask — include your Discord username. We'll delete your scores and the name/avatar stored with them. You can also simply stop using the activity; removing the app from a server stops any further collection.

8. Children

Word Train is not intended for anyone under 13, or under the minimum age required to use Discord in your country, whichever is higher.

9. Security

Identity is verified by Discord, not by us — we never see or store a password. Traffic runs over HTTPS. No system is perfectly secure, but the only data at risk here is a display name, an avatar link, and some game scores.

10. Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above changes with it.

11. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: email jonkwilson@gmail.com. Please include your Discord username so we can find your entries.
